About

P. C. Vishnunadh is the Minister for Tourism in the Government of Kerala. He is responsible for promoting Kerala tourism globally, developing tourism infrastructure, and implementing sustainable tourism initiatives across the state.

He plays a key role in strengthening Kerala’s tourism sector through branding campaigns, destination development, and policy implementation aimed at attracting domestic and international visitors.
