About

Mons Joseph is a senior political leader from Kerala who has represented the Kaduthuruthy constituency in the Kerala Legislative Assembly. He is widely recognized for his contributions to public service, infrastructure development and local governance.

Throughout his political career, he has actively worked for rural development, education, transportation improvements and welfare initiatives aimed at enhancing the quality of life of citizens.
