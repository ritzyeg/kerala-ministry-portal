About

Sunny Joseph is a senior political leader from Kerala and a Member of the Kerala Legislative Assembly representing the Peravoor constituency. He is known for his commitment to public service, legal expertise and active participation in legislative affairs.

With a background in law and public administration, he has consistently worked towards improving infrastructure, education, healthcare and welfare initiatives for the people of Kerala.
