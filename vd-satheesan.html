About

Vadassery Damodaran Satheesan is a prominent political leader from Kerala. He has represented the Paravur constituency and is known for legislative debates, governance initiatives and public welfare advocacy.

Born in Nettoor, Kochi, he pursued higher education in social work and law before entering active politics.
