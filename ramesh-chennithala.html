About

Ramesh Chennithala is one of Kerala's most experienced political leaders and a senior member of the Indian National Congress. Over several decades he has held important organizational and legislative positions at both state and national levels.

Beginning his political career through student movements, he later became a Youth Congress leader, Member of Parliament, KPCC President, Home Minister of Kerala and Leader of Opposition.

Known for his administrative experience and commitment to public welfare, he has played a significant role in Kerala's political and social development.
